Your donations support us to expand our work in Nepal, which in turn empower marginalised women to take control of their own lives. 

Our programs fall into three categories:

Education:
Literacy and Numeracy, gender awareness, Women's rights, Health  

Skills Training:
Knitting, sewing, felting 

Income generation: 
savings and loans, opening bank accounts, basic business training, micro finance programs.  

We THANK-YOU for your support.
