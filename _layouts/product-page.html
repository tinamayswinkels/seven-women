Shipping

Under normal circumstances, items are shipped within 1 working day of cleared payment received. Our preferred carrier is Australia Post. Domestic parcels normally reach within 5 working days. Overseas parcels may require upto 10 working days. FLAT AUD 5.00 shipping fee for domestic buyers!!

Refunds & Returns

All products come with a 21 day return guarantee. Any returned item must be received by us within this time. Items must be returned unused, unwashed, in their original condition and packaging with all tags intact. You may either exchange the item or request a full refund. Return postage is at the sender`s expense - should you request replacements for returned items, you will not be charged again for delivery.
