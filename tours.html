Come explore Nepal

Hands of Development tours take travellers from the bustling city of Kathmandu to the stunning mountain ranges and jungle of Chitwan National Park and the ancient city of Bhaktapur

Immersive tours to Nepal

You will further encounter a range of grassroots organisations empowering women, ending human trafficking and exploitation. Meet leaders and game changers working in diverse sectors including human rights law, education and community development. Learn firsthand what it takes to create real and lasting change in a developing country.

Hands On Development tours are perfect for those of any age or background curious to experience more of our beautiful world and gain a deeper insight into the culture and peoples of Nepal.
People leave the experience with memories of spectacular scenes of natural beauty, a profound understanding of Nepal across all levels of society and having witnessed first hand the work of charitable organisations creating real and lasting change.

So, join us to challenge your perspectives, form lasting friendships with like-minded individuals and experience a sense of joy, contribution, connection and growth on our next tour!
